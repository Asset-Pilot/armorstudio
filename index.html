Non-personal information does not identify you personally and may include:

King Studio does not directly collect personal information from users. However, some information may be collected automatically through third-party services integrated into our applications.

Third-party services may collect persistent identifiers such as advertising identifiers (for example Android Advertising ID) strictly for internal operations such as delivering and measuring advertisements.

These identifiers are used only for advertising and analytics purposes and do not personally identify users.

These technologies may collect limited non-personal information related to device usage in order to provide relevant advertisements and improve advertising services.

Analytics

Our applications may use analytics tools or built-in platform services to understand how users interact with our games. This information helps us improve gameplay, stability, and overall user experience.

In-App Purchases

Some of our applications may include optional in-app purchases. All purchases are processed securely through the official app store (such as Huawei AppGallery). We do not collect or store payment information.

Our applications do not request or intentionally collect sensitive personal information such as health data, financial information, or precise location data.
